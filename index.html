Sue Costello

Sue has appeared on countless television shows including "NYPD Blue” and "Tough Crowd with Colin Quinn” on Comedy Central. After starring in two TV pilots for CBS, Sue earned her very own self-titled television series, "Costello". She was also a guest host on NBC's "Later,” performed on Comedy Central's "Premium Blend,” and made it to the finals of NBC’s “Last Comic Standing.” She has also been seen on "Comic's Unleashed" with Byron Allen, and has appeared twice on Marc Maron’s Podcast WTF.

About this show

From sleepy bars along the Yangtze River to headlining tours through fifteen countries around Asia, in six short years the Kung Fu Komedy crew have made their mark on audiences and critics worldwide and established themselves as China’s premier comedy club.

Now the pioneers of stand-up in Asia are bringing their unique brand of comedy to New York City with new shows every month in Greenwich Village.
